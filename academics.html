THE AIA PEDAGOGY

Learning Beyond
The Textbook.

Our academic structure is built on the philosophy of Active Learning. We move beyond rote memorization to foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and digital literacy.

  • STEAM Integration: Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math are interwoven into daily lessons.
  • Dual Curriculum: We prepare students for WAEC/NECO while integrating key aspects of the Cambridge IGCSE.
  • Continuous Assessment: 40% of grades are derived from projects, presentations, and tests; 60% from terminal exams.

Entry Requirements

Junior Secondary (JSS1)

  • Minimum age of 10 years by September.
  • Common Entrance Examination Score (Maths, English, General Paper).
  • Previous academic report (Year 5/6).
  • Successful Oral Interview (Student & Parents).

Senior Secondary (SS1)

  • Completion of JSS3 (BECE/Junior WAEC results).
  • Minimum of 6 Credits including Math & English.
  • Departmental placement test (Science/Arts/Commercial).
  • Character reference letter from previous school.

Transfer Students

  • Transcripts from the last 2 academic sessions.
  • Transfer Certificate.
  • AIA Entrance Examination.
  • Note: We do not accept transfer students into terminal classes (JSS3 & SS3).
