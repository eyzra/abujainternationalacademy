Academic & Co-Curricular Life

Primary School Curriculum

Our Primary School curriculum fosters a love of learning through a blend of the Nigerian and British curricula. We focus on foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and science, while encouraging creativity through arts, music, and play-based learning.

Junior High School Curriculum

In Junior High, students transition to a more specialized subject-based curriculum. We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration, preparing students for the challenges of senior secondary education and their BECE examinations.

Senior High School Curriculum

Our Senior High program offers specialized pathways in Science, Arts, and Business. We provide comprehensive preparation for WAEC, NECO, and international examinations like IGCSE and SAT, ensuring our graduates are competitive globally.
