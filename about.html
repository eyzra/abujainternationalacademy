Our History

1997

Inception

Abuja International Academy is a private quality school established in January, 1997 by Evang. G.F.I Amu and administered by an erudite educationist Mr. J.A Imoudu (Baba).

1999

Establishment

The school was granted a formal approval by Nassarawa State Government on 5th March, 1999 and recognized as center for Senior School Certificate Examinations by both National Examination Council (NECO) and the West African Examinations Council (WAEC) in 2000 and 2004 respectively

ONGOING

Expansion
