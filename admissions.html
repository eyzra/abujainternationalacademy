Admissions

Admissions Process

  1. Enquiry & Campus Tour: Contact our admissions office to ask questions and schedule a tour of our facilities.
  2. Application Form: Purchase and complete the application form, available online or at our campuses.
  3. Entrance Examination: Prospective students will sit for a computer-based test (CBT) to assess their academic readiness.
  4. Interview: A successful examination is followed by an informal interview with the student and parents.
  5. Offer of Admission: Successful candidates will receive an offer of admission, along with details for acceptance and fee payment.
